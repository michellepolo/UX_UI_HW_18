About me

Who am I?

I am a Bolivian-born professional with a strong passion for painting, creating, and innovating. I have worked with a unique perspective that infuses my work with authenticity and originality. My academic journey began with a degree in Marketing and Advertising, which gave me a comprehensive understanding of consumer behavior and market dynamics. Expanding my skill set further, I earned a Diploma in Digital Marketing, harnessing the power of digital news to drive impactful campaigns. I recently earned a Master's in Marketing, which allowed me to hone my strategic acumen and my ability to create brand stories. I am currently doing a UX/UI bootcamp program, which has allowed me to project my entire professional career to user experience and interfaces. My portfolio reflects who I am, differentiating myself as a versatile and innovative professional in the world of UX/UI and strategic marketing.
